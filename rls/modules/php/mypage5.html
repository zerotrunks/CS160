Success Story

Patrick Ng, SJSU MSCS 2005

While studying at SJSU for my MSCS degree, I was a full-time software engineer at Cisco Systems. Although it is a challenge to balance both work and studies, it was an enjoyable experience. In order to cope with my busy work schedule, I mostly took evening classes. I did research work with Dr. Tseng on improving user satisfaction and accuracy in RSS information retrieval. Our research was published in the “Information Management & Computer Security” journal.
